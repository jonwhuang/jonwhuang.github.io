About Me

Hello! I'm a software engineer and a recent graduate from Dev Bootcamp. Before becoming enveloped in the world of programming, I graduated from UC San Diego with a B.S. in Human Biology. I loved the fast-paced atmosphere of the health care industry selflessly focused on helping out others.

Near the end of my undergraduate career, I began to feel that the idea of being a doctor was mundane. Hearing my worries, a friend nudged me towards computer science. My first introductory course to C++ made me realize the exhilarating and endless possibilities of programming. With how quick the technology industry is expanding today, there are so many new technologies to learn and problems to solve. Coding provides the perfect opportunity to build something to potentially change people's lives.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy taking walks, spending time with friends, and eating new and different foods. Please feel free to contact me about anything!
